Introducing Our Citizen-Officer Portal

As we all know that India has been dealing with hundreds of crimes daily and this has always in some way or the other and has tested the functioning of the government institutions such as the police department, crime branch, intelligence agencies etc. FIRs are registered daily every hour and this has increased the pressure in the institutions to solve these problems . Many multiple ways have been applied to the system to make the working efficient but still crimes are not getting solved. One of the main problems that we face is the reporting of the crime . As we all know that reporting of crime has to be done in the jurisdiction of the crime area. So now a solution has been generated such that crime reporting can be done from anywhere and no more limitation of being in the jurisdiction being bound.

About Mumbai Police

The Mumbai Police (Marathi: मुंबई पोलीस, IAST: Mumbaī Pulīs, formerly Bombay Police) is the police department of the city of Mumbai, Maharashtra. It is a part of Maharashtra Police and has the primary responsibilities of law enforcement and investigation within the limits of Mumbai. The force's motto is "Sadrakṣaṇāya Khalanigrahaṇāya" (Sanskrit: सद्रक्षणाय खलनिग्रहणाय, English: "To protect Good and to destroy Evil").

It is headed by the Commissioner of the Mumbai Police assisted by an IPS officer in the rank of Additional Director General, and each district headed is headed by a Deputy Commissioner of Police in the rank of Superintendent of Police (excluding jails headed by Inspector Generals). Each police station is headed by a Senior Inspector called the Station House Officer (SHO).
